Nigerian Government Hands Over Geregu Power Plant To Forte Oil

Forte Oil is pleased to annouce that the 414MW Geregu Power Plant acquired under the government-led privatization by its subsidiary, Emperion Power Distribution Company Limited has been formally handed over for full operational control in a brief ceremony held at the Aso Rock Villa in Abuja on September 30, 2012.

Dr. Good Luck Jonathan, President of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, formally handed over the certificate of ownership to Mr. Femi Otedola, Chairman, Forte Oil PLC in a ceremony that also has Arch. Namadi Sambo, the Vice President, Federal Republic of Nigeria, in attendance.
